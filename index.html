Build notes

Design and implementation decisions

A brief walkthrough to reference in a video or handoff.

Layout choices

Split hero for clarity; modular cards for Features, Use Cases, Testimonials, and Pricing to scale with CMS data.

Responsiveness

Fluid grids with minmax cards and wrap; stacks collapse on tablet/mobile; max-width containers to prevent overflow on ultrawide screens.

CMS structure

Features, Testimonials, and Blog collections use typed fields; lists render dynamically with graceful empty states.

SEO decisions

One H1, keyworded H2s (automation, productivity, workflow), meta + OG tags, descriptive alt text, clean semantic hierarchy.
